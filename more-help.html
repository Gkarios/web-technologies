How do I see my current tasklists?

+

By clicking on "Your Tasks" at the top of the page, you will be given the opportunity to search for your current tasks; whether assigned or self-created.

How do I assign a task to a colleague?

+

Below any task there is a field to add anyone you like.
As long as they have an account, just ask for their username (which is exclusive to them) and assign them the task!

What is SimplepushKey?

+

SimplepushKey is an Android App that will notify your mobile device whenever another user has assigned you with a task.

This is for optional use of course.
