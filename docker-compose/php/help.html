What is our mission?

+

Our mission is to provide you with services that allow for task management and assignment. As well as providing you with the ability to notify your colleagues and stay up to date.

How do i sign up?

+

On the front-page of the site by clicking on "Get Started" you will be redirected to a sign up form. Simply provide your first and last name, your email and a password.

Why should i use trello clone?

+

We provide you with such services free of advertisers, unlike other current apps.
Our features are straight-forward and lightweight.
We integrate 'SimplepushKey' services to notify your colleagues.
