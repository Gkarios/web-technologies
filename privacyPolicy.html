Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22/9/2024

This privacy policy describes how Trello collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit our website: [https://localhost/trello].


Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information that you provide to us directly, such as your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide. We do not have access to your password, as it is encrypted.

  • Data Usage: We may collect Personal Identifiable Information that you have given us. But we ensure that our data collection complies to GDPR regulations.

  • Right to delete: According to GDPR Article 17. A user has the right to delete their data. You can practice this right by entering the "Edit" page once you log into your account. You may delete your account, and all of the data we've collected by pressing the 'delete' button. All of your Tasks and Task-Lists will remain, unless you manually delete them.
    Should they remain, they will be saved as absolutely anonymous.
